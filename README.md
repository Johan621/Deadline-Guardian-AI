# 🛡️ Deadline Guardian AI

### AI Chief of Staff for High-Stakes Deadlines

Deadline Guardian AI is an AI-powered productivity companion designed to prevent users from missing important commitments before failure occurs.

Unlike traditional task managers and reminder apps, Deadline Guardian proactively analyzes workload, predicts deadline risk, recommends high-impact actions, simulates the consequences of delay, and generates recovery plans when users fall behind.

Built for the **Vibe2Ship Hackathon** under the **"The Last-Minute Life Saver"** problem statement.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Students, professionals, and entrepreneurs frequently miss assignments, exams, interviews, projects, and critical deadlines because existing productivity tools focus on reminders rather than action.

Deadline Guardian AI helps users make better decisions by identifying risks early and guiding them toward the most effective next step.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎯 AI Goal Intake

Users describe their commitments in natural language.

Examples:

* "DBMS exam in 5 days"
* "Placement interview next Monday"
* "Hackathon submission due this weekend"

---

### ⚠️ Failure Risk Prediction

Analyzes required effort versus available time and predicts the likelihood of missing a commitment.

Example:

* Required Preparation: 20 Hours
* Available Capacity: 14 Hours
* Failure Risk: 68%

---

### 📊 Preparation Capacity Analysis

Calculates:

* Required Hours
* Available Hours
* Capacity Deficit/Surplus
* Overall Success Probability

Status Indicators:

* OVERLOADED
* AT RISK
* SURVIVABLE

---

### 🧠 Strategic Action Sequencing

Automatically generates high-impact actions for each goal.

Example:

1. Complete Relational Algebra
2. Review Normalization
3. Solve Previous Year Questions

---

### 🔮 Delay Impact Simulator

Allows users to understand the consequences of postponing work.

Example:

* Current Risk: 35%
* If Delayed: 49%

---

### 🔄 Recovery Center

When unexpected events occur, users can rebuild their plan and recover lost progress.

Examples:

* Missed study session
* Illness
* Personal emergency
* Unexpected workload

The system restructures priorities and improves success probability.

---

### 🤖 Local Intelligence Fallback

If Gemini is unavailable, Deadline Guardian automatically switches to deterministic local analysis, ensuring uninterrupted functionality.
